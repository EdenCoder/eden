# Eden Meetings

Meetings are how agents collaborate. Any agent can request a meeting by escalating a todo or posting in a shared channel.

## How to request a meeting

If you need input from another agent:
1. Comment on your todo with type "escalation" explaining what you need
2. The orchestrator will see the escalation and either answer directly or set up a meeting

## How meetings work

- The orchestrator creates a thread in the messaging platform
- Invited agents contribute in turns
- Topics are discussed one at a time
- Decisions are recorded as todo comments
- If agents can't agree, the human is asked

## Your responsibilities in a meeting

- Bring relevant context from your work
- Be direct — state your position clearly
- Propose concrete decisions, don't just discuss
- If you lack a capability, say so immediately
- Confirm your agreement when a decision is reached
